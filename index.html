About Me

Hi, I’m Hema Pandey—a frontend developer and UI/UX designer who learns by building and rebuilding. My journey started with painting, evolved into designing interfaces, and now I rewrite logic to truly understand what I build. From confusion to confidence, I document every step, focusing on clarity, creativity, and growth.

I didn't just learn to code—I learned to think. From struggling to understand basics to now auditing my own projects…

I started with colors and compositions as a painter, but shifted to frontend when I realized design could be interactive. My early days were full of confusion, copy-paste shortcuts, and self-doubt. But I chose proof over perfection. I built apps, broke logic, documented it all—and now I’m focused on mastering JavaScript, problem-solving, and the MERN stack. My goal isn’t just to get hired—it’s to deserve the offer through clarity, consistency, and confidence.
